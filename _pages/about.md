## About

- I’m a neuroscience student focused on understanding how large-scale brain network organisation supports cognition and how it breaks down in aging and disease
- My work uses resting-state fMRI to study system-level connectivity, with an emphasis on segregation and integration across brain networks.

---

## Research Focus

- Large-scale brain networks (resting-state fMRI)
- System segregation and integration
- Network changes in aging and Alzheimer’s disease
- Network reorganisation impacts on cognition

---

## Current Work

- Analyzing resting-state functional connectivity to examine how system-level organisation (within- and between-network connectivity) changes across diagnostic groups and age.

- Using GLME models to test how disease status alters network segregation across large-scale brain systems.
